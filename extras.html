Welcome to Shariff Goat Farming

At Shariff Goat Farming, located in the heart of Garissa County, we specialize in raising healthy goats to provide high-quality meat and nutritious milk. Our farm is dedicated to sustainable farming practices that ensure the well-being of our animals and the quality of our products.

Our Products

We provide premium products from our goats:

  • Goat Meat: Tender, high-protein meat perfect for your meals.
  • Goat Milk: Fresh, creamy milk ideal for dairy products such as cheese and yogurt.

About Shariff Goat Farming

Shariff Goat Farming started with a passion for raising goats in a sustainable environment. Located in Garissa County, we focus on producing high-quality goat meat and milk while promoting the welfare of our animals. We pride ourselves on responsible farming methods that ensure the best products for our customers.
